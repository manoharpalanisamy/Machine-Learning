You must have installed Anaconda virtual envirionment or Standalone Python3 with juypter notebook installed on your PC's

The following Packagas needed to work for Twitter Sentiment Analysis

1) Python3 with Juypter-notebook
2) re - regular expressions
3) Numpy 
4) Pandas
5) Matplotlib
6) Seaborn
7) NLTK library you must download before get into
8) WordCloud
